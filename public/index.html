RAGS & LUNGS unite

In an OPEN CALL to artists and gardens to participate in the innaugural 2020 RAGS initiative.

RAGS - Residency ART in GARDENS initiative - strives to preserve and strengthen the long relationship between downtown art and green spaces by placing an artist in residence in each of the Lower East Side's community gardens.

  • KEEPING GARDENS OPEN!
  • PROMOTING LOCAL ARTISTS!
  • FOCUSING ON ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY ART!

see lungsnyc.org/rags for more info and submission forms.

Visual Artists Rights Act (VARA) gives artists full rights to work that is created or installed in any public space, the presence of newly-created art would put an extra roadblock in the way of politicians and developers eager to turn our downtown green spaces into residential or commercial buildings.

Donate to the East River Park Attack Project to support the mission to "stop the destruction of our park by creating the biggest mural in NYC with the help of local artists, nonprofits, activists and local residents of the Lower East Side and East Village."
